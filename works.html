Lost in the Sands

16/06/2022

With trembling fist,
and flowing sand.
With sweaty grip,
and near sightless eyes.

With shaky legs,
and panting lungs.
With heavy heart,
Full of gnawing thoughts.

Under desert sun,
and swearing breath.
Under weighty sins,
Trapped by mighty chains.

Stumbling,
Tumbling,
Slipping,
Grasping!

Cursed by my follies!
Bound to these dunes!
Cursed be my name!
Bound to these pains!

Once more alone!
Once more at the end!
Lost without hope!
Once more where it began!

Out of time,
Lost to these desert sands!

Out of time,
Lost once more again.



Long Abandoned

15/06/2022

Crunch, Crunch
Crunch, Crunch
A pair traverses a road long forsaken.

Thump, Thump
Thump, Thump
A couple ascends a building long deserted.

Drip, Drip
Drip, Drip
Two faces peer out a window long shattered.

Bump, Bump
Bump, Bump
Two beating hearts, two hands entwined.

Two pinpricks fall,
Three suns alight,
On a people long abandoned
