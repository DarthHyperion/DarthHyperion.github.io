Weary Mind

As I lay awake and alone
with tired eyes,
and aching back.

The ticking clock
a jackhammer
on my slipping mind.

I stare at the ceiling,
darkened and crooked,
memorizing crag and valley.

With this weary mind,
I think to myself,
Is this all there is to life?



White Green Flag

20/06/2022

In this stinking mulch,
Beneath this rotting tree,
Lies the corpse of a noble eagle,
Bleeding from it’s beak.

In this barren soil,
Among these dying roots,
Lie the graves of our fathers:
Accusing, at our feet.

On this dying bark,
Along this bloated trunk,
The names of our martyrs,
Etched in fading blood.

In this violent storm,
Atop these failing twigs,
A tattered white-green flag,
Under torrential flood.

In this sickly glade,
Cursed be their names,
Are selfish, pilfering gardeners,
Thieving as we speak.

In these dwindling leaves,
Are plagues fouler than death.
Do we cure these afflictions?
Or burn them all away?



Bathroom Breakdown

19/06/2022

Heavy breaths,
Stained hands,
Spittle and iron upon my lips.

“Loathsome fool!”
“Insolent whelp!”
An accusing visage stares back.

Weak knees,
Sinking low,
A roiling twist deep within.

Boiling rage,
Grasping hands,
The mirror shatters in twain.

Bubbling sobs,
Shaking free,
And where two eyes watched,
Now stared a hundred and three.



Lost in the Sands

18/06/2022

With trembling fist,
and flowing sand.
With sweaty grip,
and near sightless eyes.

With shaky legs,
and panting lungs.
With heavy heart,
Full of gnawing thoughts.

Under desert sun,
and swearing breath.
Under weighty sins,
Trapped by mighty chains.

Stumbling,
Tumbling,
Slipping,
Grasping!

Cursed by my follies!
Bound to these dunes!
Cursed be my name!
Bound to these pains!

Once more alone!
Once more at the end!
Lost without hope!
Once more where it began!

Out of time,
Lost to these desert sands!

Out of time,
Lost once more again.



Long Abandoned

17/06/2022

Crunch, Crunch
Crunch, Crunch
A pair traverses a road long forsaken.

Thump, Thump
Thump, Thump
A couple ascends a building long deserted.

Drip, Drip
Drip, Drip
Two faces peer out a window long shattered.

Bump, Bump
Bump, Bump
Two beating hearts, two hands entwined.

Two pinpricks fall,
Three suns alight,
On a people long abandoned
