Lost in the Sands

With trembling fist,
and flowing sand.
With sweaty grip,
and near sightless eyes.

With shaky legs,
and panting lungs.
With heavy heart,
Full of gnawing thoughts.

Under desert sun,
and swearing breath.
Under weighty sins,
Trapped by mighty chains.

Stumbling,
Tumbling,
Slipping,
Grasping!

Cursed by my follies!
Bound to these dunes!
Cursed be my name!
Bound to these pains!

Once more alone!
Once more at the end!
Lost without hope!
Once more where it began!

Out of time,
Lost to these desert sands!

Out of time,
Lost once more again.
